Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Brealle (“Brealle,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you visit our website, contact us, or interact with our systems and communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details.
  • Inquiry or application information that you submit through forms, email, SMS, or other communications.
  • Usage information, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and general analytics data.
  • Workflow and communication data needed to operate follow-up, scheduling, intake, or support processes.

2. How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide services.
  • Operate, test, improve, and maintain automation workflows and business systems.
  • Send service-related, operational, application-related, or follow-up communications.
  • Provide updates regarding scheduling, onboarding, support, or next steps.
  • Monitor site usage and improve website performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and security obligations.

3. SMS and Messaging

If you provide a phone number through a form, application, inquiry, or direct interaction, we may use that number to send operational, non-promotional text messages related to your request, application, scheduling, workflow status, or next steps.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers and tools used to operate our business, such as website hosting, analytics, email, communications, scheduling, payment, CRM, or automation platforms, only as reasonably necessary to run our services and operations.

Mobile information, including phone numbers, will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed for legitimate business purposes, to provide services, maintain records, improve systems, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • You may request that we update or delete certain personal information, subject to legal or operational requirements.
  • You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • You may opt out of non-essential email communications by following the unsubscribe instructions, when applicable.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website and workflows may use third-party services and integrations. Those services may have their own privacy policies and practices. We encourage you to review them where relevant.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
